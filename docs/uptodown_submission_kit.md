# Nexus Jobs Scanner - Uptodown Submission Kit

## 1) Clear App Description

### Short Description
Nexus Jobs Scanner helps students and early-career candidates discover internship openings by scanning company career pages and aggregating structured results in one app.

### Full Description
Nexus Jobs Scanner is a native Android app built for internship discovery.

Users upload a CSV/XLSX list of company career URLs, configure keyword filters (include/exclude), and run a scan. Nexus extracts listings from ATS systems and career pages, then organizes results into clear tabs:
- Internships (matches)
- No Listings
- Errors

The app includes practical native features:
- Local bookmarks for saving jobs
- Offline-saved mode to review bookmarked jobs without re-scanning
- Export results to CSV
- Scan progress and per-company status tracker
- Home widget for quick scan access

Who this app is for:
- Students searching internships
- Career hunters tracking Web3/tech companies
- Recruiters/researchers monitoring internship pages
