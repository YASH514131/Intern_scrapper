# Nexus Jobs Scanner Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-13

## Overview
Nexus Jobs Scanner helps users aggregate internship listings from company career pages.

## Data We Process
- Company list data uploaded by the user (CSV/XLSX)
- Scan configuration (keywords, exclude terms, scan limits)
- Job listing metadata retrieved from public job pages
- Optional app analytics events (non-sensitive telemetry)

## What We Do Not Request
- No account sign-up is required
- No password or credential collection in-app
- No resume file upload inside the app

## Local Storage
The app stores the following data locally on the device:
- Saved/bookmarked jobs for offline review
- Local scan state and app preferences

## External Links
When users open a job listing, they are redirected to the original company website.

## Third-Party Services
- Firebase Analytics may be used for aggregate usage telemetry
- No intentionally collected personal identifiers are sent by app event names

## Data Retention
Local app data remains on-device until the user clears app data or uninstalls the app.
